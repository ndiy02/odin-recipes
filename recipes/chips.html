Chips

Description

Golden fried chips with ketchup and mayonaise.

Ingredients

  • Crispy potatoes
  • Oil (for frying potatoes)
  • Salt (for seasoning)
  • Pepper (for seasoning)

Steps

  1. Heat oil to 375F.
  2. Cut potatoes into thin stripes,dry.
  3. Fry in batches,2-3 minutes each.
  4. Drain,season, enjoy with dips!
